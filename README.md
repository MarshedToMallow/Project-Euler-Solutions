# Project Euler Solutions

A collection of my solutions to Project Euler problems 1 to 100.

The guidelines on their website state that the solutions for problems outside the first 100 should not be shared and I think that's exceedingly reasonable.

The solutions here are organized in groups of 10 and aim to showcase my own efforts and provide a guide into problem solving strategies.

I encourage anyone here looking for answers to make a genuine attempt to reach the answers for themselves without my own solutions.

I can verify that solving these problems myself has been incredibly rewarding and I'd hope no one deprives themselves the chance to feel that accomplishment.
